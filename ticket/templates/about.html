About us

Shopwave was founded on August, 2020 during the pandemic. The C.E.O. knew the country was at a state of emergency, and everyone weren't allowed to move out of their houses. So he came up with the idea of an ecommerce website to help his community.

How to shop on our page

How to buy on Shopwave?

1. Search for the item. Find what you need using search panel and filters. We have over a million adverts, choose exactly what you are looking for.

2. Click on item. After clicking on an item, you will be directed to the product detail page. It is for you add to cart and view product description 3. Take your item or order a delivery.

We check your order carefully, but it’s always better to check twice, right? Meet a seller in public place and be sure to pay only after collecting your item.

4. Leave your feedback about out servuce on twitter, instagram and facebook. Feel free to tell us about your purchase. Your feedback will be published online on the seller’s page and will be very helpful for other buyers. Let’s build a safe and professional business community together!
How to sell on Shopwave?
1. Register
Register using your e-mail and phone number (or do it via Facebook or Google). Make sure you’re entering a correct phone number, so your clients could reach you!

2. Make photos of your item. Feel free to make a lot of photos using your smartphone. Make sure they show your item in the best light.

3. Press SELL . Choose a proper category, upload your photos and write a clear title and full description of your item. Enter a fair price, select attributes and send your advert to review!

4. Answer the messages and calls from your clients! If everything is ok with your advert, it’ll be on Jiji in a couple of hours after sending to moderation. We’ll send you a letter and notification when your advert goes live. Check your messages and be ready to earn money!

Safety

1. General We are highly focused on the security and can solve any issues in short-terms. That’s why we ask you, kindly, to leave a review after purchasing. If you run into any problems with a seller, you can report us and Shopwave Team will check this seller as soon as possible.

2. Personal safety tips. - Do not pay in advance, even for the delivery - Try to meet at a safe, public location - Check the item BEFORE you buy it - Pay only after collecting the item

3. Secure payments. Jiji provides Premium Services for those who want to sell and earn more. We accept both online and offline payments for these services. We guarantee secure and reliable payments on Shopwave.
